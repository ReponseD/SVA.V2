Our Journey in Robotics

We first came together through our participation in the FLL competition in Rwanda, where we discovered our shared passion for robotics and problem-solving. Inspired by what we learned, we decided to take the next step and form Lyrc - Phos Khoinonia.

As students from different schools across Rwanda, we meet regularly to design, build, and program robots that can solve real-world challenges. Our name "Phos Khoinonia" means "Light of Fellowship" in Greek, reflecting our belief that innovation happens best when we work together.

Our FRC 2026 Dream

We're preparing to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition, representing Rwanda on the global stage.
