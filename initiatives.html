Our Initiatives

Lyrc: Phos Khoinonia

We are building a full-scale robot to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) 2026 — one of the most prestigious robotics events in the world.

As high school students from across Rwanda, we’re learning engineering, coding, and teamwork to represent Africa on the global stage.

  • FRC 2026 Dream
  • STEM Education Focus
  • Pan-African Representation

MyDoctor AI

A student-built AI tool designed to help diagnose common health issues in local communities using Swahili and Kinyarwanda.

MyDoctor AI aims to bridge the gap between rural populations and medical knowledge by providing accessible, language-native health guidance.

  • Built by Rwandan Students
  • Multilingual (Kinyarwanda, Swahili)
  • Open-Source & Free

SVA XPozd

A youth-powered talent show and podcast series that highlights innovation, music, culture, and social themes — like “Avoid Abolition in Youth.”

Hosted by SVA members, SVA XPozd gives young voices a platform to speak about tech, creativity, and what it means to be African in the 21st century.

  • Talent Shows & Podcasts
  • Youth Hosts & Guests
  • Themes: Innovation, Culture, Social Change
