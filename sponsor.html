Why Sponsor Lyrc?

We are high school students from different schools across Rwanda. In 2024, we started with the FLL competition in Kigali. That experience changed everything.

Now, we’re building a full-scale robot to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) in 2026 — one of the most prestigious robotics events in the world.

We don’t have a fancy lab or big sponsors — yet. But we have something stronger: passion, purpose, and the courage to try.
